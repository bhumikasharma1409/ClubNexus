Welcome to IEEE

IEEE is one of the most active technical clubs in our college, dedicated to promoting innovation, coding, and technology among students. We organize coding competitions, hackathons, technical workshops, and expert talks to help members enhance their skills and stay updated with the latest trends in technology. Our club provides a collaborative environment for students of all levels to learn, share, and grow together.

Join IEEE to connect with tech enthusiasts, participate in exciting events, and build a strong foundation for your future in technology!
