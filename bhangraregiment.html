The Bhangra Regiment Student Chapter is a vibrant community that celebrates the joy, rhythm, and energy of Punjab’s traditional dance form — Bhangra!

This club provides a stage for dancers to express themselves, perform with passion, and promote cultural pride. Through workshops, performances, and competitions, members enhance their confidence, coordination, and team spirit.

Join us and feel the beat of tradition — where every step tells a story!
